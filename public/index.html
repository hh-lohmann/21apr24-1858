Crawling through ReactJS

This is meant to have some smallest demos for smallest concepts one might (will) encounter in ReactJS. No ambitioned endeavor, nothing to learn new, no cheat sheet, no claim to completeness of any kind.

In the remainder we will say "React", after intentionally using "ReactJS" in the beginning.

Some fancy goals that combine technical demo with immediate value may include a component that generates a TOC right here.
